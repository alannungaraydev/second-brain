
## Leadership and Managerial Competencies 

Objectives
- Detect the existence of managerial competencies in myself and colleagues 
- Develop strategic thinking and implement business intelligence tools 
- Learn different negotiation strategies 
- Understand different leadership theories and styles for adoption.


### Managerial Competencies

Managerial competencies improve the individual performance of a leader. There are three main areas to evaluate these managerial competencies.

1.- Ethics and values: integrity and respect must be behind any action and decision of a leader.
- Assume responsibilities and not blame third parties
- Respect for diversity of opinions and beliefs
- Zero tolerance for abuse
- Show respect (do not show cynical or aggressive communication or behavior).
- Be Impartial
- Place objectives ahead of personal goals
- Do not let emotions interfere with judgment.

2.- Strategic thinking: innovation through analysis and ideas must be reinforced with managerial competencies.
- Analysis
	- Has a wide range of opinions
	- Does not exclude participants
	- Acts on circumstances 
	- Is empathetic with context.
- Ideas:
	- Vision 
	- Is a guide
	- Designs short-term responses taking into account long-term consequences
	- Designs long-term plans taking into account short-term consequences.
	- Understands the impact of a strategy that is too detail-oriented or too abstract.
